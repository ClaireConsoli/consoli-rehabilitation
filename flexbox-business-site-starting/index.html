The Consoli Rescue and Rehabilitation Center

Our Mission

We are a small family with a big heart dedicated to the welfare of Australia's native animals.
Our mission is to follow the Three Rs of Wildlife Care.
We Rescue, Rehabilitate, and Release the animals that come into our care.

From the smallest potoroo to the largest Red Kangaroo, we treat each one with love, tenderness, and respect.
